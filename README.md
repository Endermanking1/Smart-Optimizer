# Smart-Optimizer

Smart Optimizer Stop lag before it starts.

Most optimization plugins just "kill" entities and call it a day. This one actually watches your hardware. If your RAM usage spikes or your TPS dips, Smart Optimizer dynamically scales back your simulation distance and throttles heavy entity tasks. Once your server catches its breath, it pushes your settings back up so the game stays looking great.

Live Hardware Monitoring: Real-time RAM & CPU-time tracking.

Smart Scaling: Adjusts render/sim distance based on load.

Zero Visual Sacrifice: Keeps your world looking full until things actually get critical.
